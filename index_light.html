Who We Are

Innovation Idea: Quill Dynamics innovation involves using a CNC pen plotter machine to draw like a human using a regular pen and paper.

Product/Service: The company offers a unique service where custom portraits and memorable moments are created on paper using the CNC pen plotter and then shipped to customers.

Team Members: The key team members include Siddhant Sundar (Project Manager, Software Lead), Madhu D (Chief Technical Officer), and V Sai Kavshik (Managing Director, CFO).

Business Plan: Quill Dynamics plans to establish a presence in cafes and educational institutions by installing vending machines offering customized portraits. The long-term vision is to extend this service nationally.
